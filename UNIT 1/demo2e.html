About Us

Craving something warm, sweet, and a little nostalgic? Our poffertjes are made fresh, fluffy, and just the way you like them — with classic powdered sugar and butter or loaded with fun toppings. Whether you’re stopping by with family, friends, or flying solo, The Poffertjes Restaurant is your go-to spot for cozy vibes and delicious Dutch treats.
