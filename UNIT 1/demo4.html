About Toby

Toby was born on February 14th, 2022 in Chilliwack, British Columbia. At 8 weeks old, he met his mom,
Rowan Salverda and moved to Victoria, British Columbia. Rowan and Toby currently live with Toby's aunt,
Ashlinn Salverda, and they all live in an apartment in Saanich, not too far from the University of Victoria.

Breed

Toby is a Havanese x Cockapoo. He is black and white, with a giant pink tongue. He has mostly Cockerspaniel
and Havanese characteristics, and barely looks like a poodle. He has very short legs, but a huge personality to make
up for his size.

He weighs about 23 lbs and is very compact. He is sturdy and can knock someone down if he runs too quickly.
He is a very cute dog with a huge personality.

Toby's Family

Toby's mom

Toby's mom is Rowan Salverda, a Dutch-Canadian who was born in Arnhem and now resides in Saanich,
British Columbia. She is 23 years old, has blonde hair, blue eyes and loves swimming and skiing.

Toby's mom loves travelling and loves to go paddleboarding with Toby.

Toby's aunt

Toby's aunt is Ashlinn Salverda. She is Rowan's younger sister and lives with Toby and Rowan in Victoria.
Toby loves to snuggle with Ashlinn and take naps with her.
