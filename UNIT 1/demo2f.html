About Ed Sheeran

Ed Sheeran is a British singer-songwriter known for emotional lyrics and acoustic pop hits like “Shape of You” and “Thinking Out Loud.” He rose to fame with his 2011 debut album “+” and became one of the world's top-selling artists. Sheeran is also known for using a loop pedal in live performances and writing songs for other major musicians.

Ed Sheeran's Family

Ed Sheeran comes from a creative and supportive family. His parents, John and Imogen Sheeran, are both artists—his father is an art curator and lecturer, while his mother is a jewelry designer. Ed has one older brother, Matthew Sheeran, who is a classical composer.

Ed is married to Cherry Seaborn, his childhood friend and long-time partner. The couple has two daughters, Lyra Antarctica and Jupiter. Ed often credits his family for keeping him grounded and inspiring his music.
